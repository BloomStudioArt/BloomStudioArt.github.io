# Tree of Life – 2026

Tree of Life is an interactive installation driven by a custom 10-foot treelike sculpture with embedded sound and lights. The sonics and lighting of the sculpture react to how participants navigate the installation space. Audiences are invited to explore collective expression through this centralized output of real-time music and lighting, building novel sensory experiences based on collaboration and collective interplay.
